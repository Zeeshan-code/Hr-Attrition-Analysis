# HR Analytics — Employee Attrition Analysis
## Overview
Analysis of 1,470 employee records to identify key drivers of attrition.
## Tools: Python, MySQL, Power BI
## Key Findings
- Overall attrition rate was 16.12% across 1,470 employees
- Sales department had the highest attrition at 20.63%, followed by HR at 19.05%
- Sales Representatives had the highest role-level attrition at 39.76%
- Employees who left earned $2,045 less per month on average than those who stayed ($4,787 vs $6,832)
- Youngest employees (18-25) had the highest attrition rate at 34.78%
- Employees who left had on average 2.24 fewer years at the company (5.13 vs 7.37 years)
